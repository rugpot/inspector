RugPot Inspector & Recovery is a fully client-side, open-source tool that reads RugPot protocol state directly from the Solana blockchain. No backend, no tracking, no analytics. The only outbound connection is to your chosen Solana RPC endpoint.

Read-only mode: Enter any wallet address to inspect positions across rounds.
Wallet mode: Connect Phantom/Solflare/Backpack to sell shares, claim reflections, claim jackpots, or close positions.

Privy / Social Login users: If you signed up on rugpot.io with email or social login, your wallet is managed by Privy. To use this tool: export your private key from rugpot.io wallet settings or from home.privy.io → import it into Phantom or Solflare → then connect here. Never share your private key with anyone.
